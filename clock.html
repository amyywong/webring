9 AM

10 years-old

you got called to the principle’s office for punching Timmy for stealing your animal crackers.
Summer of seventh grade you’re forced to move to New York because of your dad’s new job. You are not happy about leaving your friends.
Living in a new, close minded town, it was hard to make new friends. After losing your home friends, you started to believe that everything is temporary so you lost the desire to get close to people.
Freshman year of highschool you meet your best friend Rachel. You and Rachel are inseparable. Until she told the whole grade about your crush on Scott. She’s a bitch.
So. Scott does not like you back and decides to avoid you for the rest of his life. Yikes. Your first heartbreak.
Junior Year of highschool you find your passion for writing. Because you grew up not having the ability to trust anyone, you only trusted the paper and pencil with your thoughts.
You get ridicuously drunk at a house party because it is your are finally socializing before you graduate highschool. You kiss a boy with really weird hair. You don’t even remember his name. ~first kisses~.
Studying journalism in college, you are finally doing what you love. Still trying hard to make friends and let people in but you feel stable in your life for once.
You catch your dad cheating on your mom when you go back home for Thanksgiving. You tell mom. You help her burn his clothes and dip his tooth brush in the toilet. And you stop believing in love.
You start looking for jobs and you finally find a well paid internship at a mazgazine company. So, now you can stop relying on your dad.

Unfortunately, this is the year your grandma gets diagnosed with breast cancer.

Being around older, toxic men in the publication industry, you see them for who they truly are and continue to believe that all men are like that.

Mom calls you to tell you that grandma passed away.

Your 20s bring you countless of failed relationships because of your committment issues and fears of intimacy.

Alex makes you feel loved but you push him away. And you lose him. Your first real heartbreak.

First time travelling with your friends. And first time going to Malaysia to write a creative piece for your job.

You tried Pan Mee for the first time in Luala Lumpur

Travelling and stepping out of your comfort zone makes you realize that you need to stop being afraid of the world and let your guard down.
After travelling Asia for a few years, you move to New York City and buy your own apartment in East Village. You also adopt a dog because you hate children and you’re tryiing to change that.
Your article on concerntration camps in China gets published. Your friends throw you a celebration party. It is also the night you meet John who surprisingly gets your sarcastic humor and you’re not really used to overly- affectionate people but it rubs off on you.
